About

I'm a Technical Support Engineer and full-stack developer based in Montréal, with a background that sits at the intersection of deep technical debugging, developer infrastructure, and cross-functional communication. I don't just solve tickets — I build the systems, documentation, and tooling that prevent them from recurring.

At Mediaclip, I reduced first response time by 76% in three months and built the support infrastructure from scratch: Postman workspaces, knowledge bases, Freshdesk systems, and client-facing docs. At Virtuose, I shipped full-stack features across Vue.js, Django, and React, and was personally recommended to join a new team.

I work best where product depth meets technical ownership — SaaS integrations, API ecosystems, developer-facing systems. I care about making things robust, well-documented, and easier for the next person.
