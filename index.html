About

I'm a support engineer and full-stack developer based in Quebec, Canada. I came into tech from a customer service background — and I think that mix is what makes this work interesting to me. I know what frustration looks like from the user's side, and I'm learning every day how to solve it from the technical side.

At Mediaclip, I worked on turning scattered client docs into a clearer integration guide to help B2B operators navigate complex APIs. At Virtuose Technologies, I got to ship full-stack features across Vue.js, Django, and React — every project stretched me and taught me something new.

I'm drawn to SaaS integrations, API ecosystems, and developer-facing tools. There's always more to learn, and I genuinely enjoy the process. I want to build things that are solid, well-documented, and easier for whoever touches them next.
