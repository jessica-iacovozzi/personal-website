About

I'm a Technical Support Specialist and full-stack developer based in Canada, with a background that sits at the intersection of deep technical debugging, developer infrastructure, and cross-functional communication. I don't just solve tickets — I build the systems, documentation, and tooling that prevent them from recurring.

At Mediaclip, I transformed scattered client documentation into a structured integration resource, reducing friction for B2B operators navigating a complex SaaS API. At Virtuose Technologies, I shipped full-stack features across Vue.js, Django, and React, and was personally recommended to join a new team.

I work best where product depth meets technical ownership — SaaS integrations, API ecosystems, developer-facing systems. I care about making things robust, well-documented, and easier for the next person.
